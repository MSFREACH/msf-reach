About MSF REACH

MSF REACH is an ongoing collaboration between MSF and Urban Systems and Environments Resarch Group LLP, with Across the Cloud Pty Ltd assisting USER Group.

The objective of this partnership is to effectively and efficiently combine institutional data with crowd-sourced information (including social media, instant messaging, and relevant RSS feeds) in real-time, thereby equipping MSF with virtual eyes on the ground. REACH will leverage Artificial Intelligence [AI] as a mode of assisting human agents and operators; AI- assistance can automate time-consuming tasks, aid in data collection and management, enhance user capacities and capabilities, and ensure MSF emergency specialists focus on complex analysis and decision-making. With the further integration of comprehensive machine-learning [ML] processes, REACH has the potential to improve emergency monitoring and response performance in the region and beyond.
